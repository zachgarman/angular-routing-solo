Gizmo

Gizmo is a mogwai, a cute, cuddly, adorable critter with nasty habit of spawning other Mogwais when wet. Mogwai is Cantonese for "monster", which is exactly what happens when you let them eat after midnight.

Follow the Rules:

  1. Don't get them wet.
  2. No food after midnight.
  3. No sunlight.
